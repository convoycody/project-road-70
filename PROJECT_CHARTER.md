Purpose
	•	We measure public road conditions and infrastructure behavior.
	•	We do not measure people or vehicles.

What We Collect
	•	Accelerometer & gyro (aggregated)
	•	Speed bands (derived)
	•	Coarse grid location (on-device)
	•	Time buckets

What We Never Collect
	•	Names
	•	Plates
	•	VINs
	•	Faces
	•	Audio
	•	Video
	•	Exact GPS traces
	•	Identifiers of any kind

Data Quality Rules
	•	Every sample gets a confidence score.
	•	Low-confidence data is stored but excluded from analytics and rewards.

Transparency
	•	Rules are public.
	•	Changes require a version bump and published amendment.
